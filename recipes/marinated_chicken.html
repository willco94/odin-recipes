Marinated Chicken Recipe

Description

Marinating your meat is a great way to get extra flavour, and can be done in batches!

Ingredients

  • Chicken fillets
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic (or Garlic Granules)
  • Lemon Juice
  • Honey
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, one or two minced cloves of garlic, a squirt of lemon juice and honey. Primarily olive oil, but with just below equal part honey.
  2. Once mixed and properly seasoned to taste, add the chicken into the marinade. Ensure that the marinade is covering the chicken.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and refridgerate for 1-4 hours.
  4. After your desired marinating time, heat a large pan to medium heat and put the chicken in.
  5. Slowly cook the chicken for about 10-15 minutes, depending on quantity.
  6. In the final minutes of cooking, raise the heat to near maximum and sear the chicken. This will add colour and enhance flavour.
  7. Remove from pan. Either enjoy as part of a meal, or refridgerate/freeze for later!
