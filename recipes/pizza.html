Pizza Recipe

Description

Pepperoni pizza is delicious as hell, and the perfect dish for many occaisions. You make it with a flour dough, mozzarella cheese and your pepperoni of choice!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperonis

Steps

  1. Pre-head oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Toss and knead pizza dough to your desired circumference.
  3. Add one ladle-full of sauce to the dough and spread liberally.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over top to your desired cheesiness.
  5. Spread your desired level of pepperoni-ness over the pizza pie.
  6. Place into the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese starts to brown.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes (or not, if you enjoy searing your mouth), and enjoy!
