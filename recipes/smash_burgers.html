Smash Burgers Recipe

Description

Smash burgers rely on a ripping hot grill and a smash meat patty to quickly cook the burgers and get a lovely crisp to them.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Burger buns
  • Optional: Butter (to toast buns)
  • Any toppings and sauces desired

Steps

  1. Lightly oil a cast iron skillet, or other pan of choice.
  2. Place pan on a high heat until smoking. Form ground beef into balls just over 1 inch in diameter.
  3. Once the oil is smoking, put the beef balls on the pan and smash using a spatula.
  4. Let the beef cook until almost totally brown on the sides then flip. Due to the high heat, it should only take 15-30 seconds.
  5. OPTIONAL: Melt butter into another pan and toast the insides of the buns for about 1-2 minutes.
  6. Melt any cheese desired on the patty after flipping.
  7. Scrape off the grill onto the waiting burger bun, add desired toppings and condiments, and enjoy!
